airfryer-chicken

Description

Airfryers have changed cooking. This is such a healthy way to cook chicken or any other dish for that matter.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts: Buying skinless, boneless make it super easy.
  • Olive Oil: Perfect mild flavored oil for the air fryer
  • Italian Seasoning: Perfect combination of spices
  • Garlic Powder: Garlic adds that nice kick of flavor
  • Paprika: Adds color and flavor
  • Salt and Pepper: Add to taste
  • Optional: Brussel sprouts

Steps

  1. Place the chicken in the fryer and rub with the olive oil to coat
  2. In a small bowl combine the spices and rub generously on the chicken
  3. Bake at 360 for 9 min
  4. Flip (add brussel sprouts if using) Cook for another 9 min
