Braaibroodjies

Description

No South African Braai would be complete without a traditional Braaibroodjie. Quick, simple and tasty as anything.

Ingredients

  • 12 Slices of White Bread
  • 12 Slices Cheese
  • 2 Large Onions Cut in Rings
  • 3 Tomatoes Sliced
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Get yourself a beer and tell your wife that you are going to start the fire
  2. Spread one side of each slice of bread with butter.
  3. Place half the slices buttered side down on a cutting board or bbq grid.
  4. Add cheese, tomato, onion, salt and pepper.n
  5. Top each sandwich with the other slice of bread with the butter on the outside.
  6. Add the Braaibroodjies in a grid that can close.
  7. Braai over mild coals
  8. regularly turning the grid until both sides of the toasty is golden brown
